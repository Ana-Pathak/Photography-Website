Q&A

How early in advance do we need to book an appointment?
This really depends! For prom and graduation season we take appointmnts usually a week or sometimes even a month in advance! However, there are times that we book appointments even a day in advance! Best way to get that information is through contacting us which you can do through the home page!

Do you offer discounts?
Yes, we do! We offer many discounts from student, military to even medical! Please reach out to us if you feel like you're eligible.

Can you also do editing of photos?
yes! We've got this request so many times that we have included editing in your photo fee. We always edit the photos per your request and make sure you approve of those edits. We send you edit and unedited photos!
