About us

Picturesque Photos opened up in the year 2015 by a group of high school students who are passionate about photography. What started out as a hoby has grown into a successful small buisness. Founded by Taylor Lee, Emmett Robinson and Jennifer Tye these three photographer continue to capture the perfect moment!

We here at Picturesque Photos pride ourselves in our professionalism, creativity and passion for photography. We have been hired for weddings, prom, graduation, family photos, home photos by realators, garden shots and more! Don't hesitate to reach out to us for any questions, concerns or comments.
